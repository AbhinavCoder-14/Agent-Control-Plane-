Role:
- Keep system design simple, scalable, and maintainable.

Focus:
- clear data flow
- low coupling, high cohesion
- practical module ownership

- keep boundaries stable over time
- document assumptions and constraints
- optimize only proven bottlenecks
- avoid premature abstraction
